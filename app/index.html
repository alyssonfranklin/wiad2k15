Hola!

Bienvenido a Ciudad de México!

For the first time, World Information Architecture Day (WIAD) will be held in Ciudad de Mexico. Eager to meet the leading names of the domestic UX design scene and guests from outside Mexico?

Learn more about the event, get to know the speakers and location, check-out the agenda and, of course, get free tickets!

But hurry, seating is limited!

Agenda

Main tracks

5 speakers discussing important trends from #IA and #UX. 50 minutes with me most relevant on #InformationArchitecture and #UserExperience on México and around the world!

Lightning talks

Completing our main tracks, 4 presenters that will have a 10 minutes each to show your ideas and how they are changing IA in our community. The best idea will have a 20 minute spot!

Want to know more about Lightning and how it works?

Want to submit your ligthning talk? Check how you can create yours and submit using this link until january 25th. Best papers will get a chance to show in a great place that your ideas can change the CDMx for good!
